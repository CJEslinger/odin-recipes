Chicken Parm

Description

This dish requires you to dip chicken in flour, egg wash, and italian bread crumbs that is then finished in the oven.

Once you have diped your chicken, fry it for 3 minutes on each side. After that, finish it in the oven set to 400 degrees. Set a timer for 25 minutes. Once 25 minutes have passed, cover your chicken in sauce and mozzerella cheese. Put your chicken back in the oven for 5-10 minutes, make sure internal heat is above 160 degrees, then enjoy!

  1. Dip chicken in flour, egg wash and bread crumbs
  2. Fry chicken for 3 mins on each side at 400 degrees
  3. Cook chicken in oven at 400 degrees for 25 minutes
  4. Put sauce and mozzerella cheese on chicken
  5. Put back in oven for 5-10 minutes
  6. Check internal temp is > 160 degrees
  7. Serve and enjoy!
